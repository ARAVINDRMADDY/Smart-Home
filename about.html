Who We Are

Founded in 2016, Groww Smart began with a simple mission: to make smart home technology accessible, reliable, and secure for everyone. What started as a small team of three installers has grown into the region's premier home automation firm.

We don't just install gadgets; we design ecosystems. Our approach combines engineering precision with aesthetic sensibility, ensuring that your technology blends seamlessly into your home's design.

  • Certified Control4 & Crestron Installers
  • 24/7 Dedicated Support Team
  • Custom Privacy & Security Protocols

Our Mission

To enhance the quality of life for our clients by providing intuitive, reliable, and secure smart home solutions that save time and energy.

Our Vision

To be the standard-bearer for smart living, creating homes that anticipate needs and adapt to the families living within them.

Our Values

Integrity in our work, transparency in our pricing, and an unwavering commitment to customer privacy and data security.
