Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, installation services, pricing, and how smart home automation works.

General Questions

A smart home ecosystem connects various devices (lights, locks, thermostats, cameras) to a central hub and a single app. This allows them to communicate with each other and lets you control everything from one place, rather than using ten different apps.

Yes, a stable internet connection is crucial for remote access and for devices to communicate with cloud services (like voice assistants). However, our local hub installations ensure that essential automation (like scheduled lights) continues to work even if the internet goes down temporarily.

Installation Questions

Basic packages (Starter) usually take 4-6 hours to install and configure. Larger, whole-home systems (Ultimate) can take 2-3 days depending on the complexity of wiring and the number of devices. We always provide a timeline estimate after the initial consultation.

We prioritize wireless and retro-fit solutions to minimize damage to your walls. Most smart switches replace existing ones using current wiring. However, if you opt for in-ceiling speakers or hardwired ethernet, some drilling and patching may be required, which we handle professionally.

Pricing Questions

Basic usage of the app and local hub automation is completely free. We do offer optional premium subscriptions for extended video cloud storage (for security cameras) and advanced 24/7 technical support, but these are not required for core functionality.

Yes! We offer flexible financing options for larger whole-home automation projects. You can discuss EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) plans with our sales team during your initial consultation.

Warranty Details

All hardware is covered by standard manufacturer warranties (typically 1 to 3 years depending on the brand). Additionally, Groww Smart Automation provides a 1-year warranty on our installation workmanship. If any wiring or mounting fails within the first year, we'll fix it at no extra cost.

Yes, extended warranty and maintenance packages can be purchased annually. These packages include bi-annual system checkups, priority support, and hardware replacement discounts.

App & Technical Support

We integrate your devices into a unified smart home platform, usually via Apple HomeKit, Google Home, SmartThings, or our proprietary Groww Smart Dashboard. You will have one primary app to control all your installed devices.

First, try rebooting the device or checking the app for firmware updates. If the issue persists, you can open a support ticket via your dashboard. We will troubleshoot remotely or send a technician to assist you.

Contact Support

Our support team typically responds to email and dashboard tickets within 24 hours. If you are on a priority support plan, you have access to a dedicated phone line with near-instant technical assistance during business hours.

For urgent issues (e.g., smart lock failure or critical security camera outage), please call our emergency support line directly at +91 98765 43210 rather than sending an email.
